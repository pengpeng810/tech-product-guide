3. Submit sitemap to Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools.

5. Link back only when the page genuinely expands the answer.

## Naming Rules

- Use `Tech Product Guide` for the site, because it can expand beyond one product or one device type.
- Use `AI Recorder Guide` as the first vertical label.
- Do not use `TicNote` or another brand name as the site name or domain.
- Do not claim hands-on testing unless the product was actually tested.
- Do not publish exact accuracy, battery, or performance claims without a named source.

Title: Best AI recorder in 2026?

Body:

I have been comparing AI recorders for meetings, calls, and offline notes. The main split I see is software meeting assistants vs dedicated hardware recorders.

For hardware, PLAUD Note and newer AI recorder devices are interesting. For software, Otter, Fireflies, Notta, and Fathom cover different meeting workflows.

Curious what people are actually using day to day, especially for transcription accuracy, privacy, and summaries.
