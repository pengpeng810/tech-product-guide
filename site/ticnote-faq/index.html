Core Questions

What is TicNote?

TicNote is a portable AI voice recorder from Mobvoi that records conversations and turns them into transcripts, summaries, meeting minutes, translations, mind maps, and searchable notes.

Who makes TicNote?

TicNote is associated with Mobvoi, a company known for AI products and TicWatch smartwatches.

What is TicNote used for?

TicNote is used for recording meetings, phone calls, lectures, interviews, voice memos, field notes, and other spoken conversations that users want to transcribe, summarize, organize, or search later.

Is TicNote an AI voice recorder?

Yes. TicNote is an AI voice recorder because it combines audio recording hardware with AI features such as transcription, summarization, translation, meeting minutes, and note organization.

How much storage does TicNote have?

Official public materials describe TicNote as having 64GB of local storage.

How many languages does TicNote support?

TicNote is marketed with support for 120+ languages in transcription and translation workflows.

Is TicNote a PLAUD Note alternative?

Yes. TicNote can be considered a PLAUD Note alternative because both are portable AI voice recorders that capture conversations and produce transcripts and summaries.

Can TicNote record phone calls?

TicNote is marketed with a phone-call recording mode. Users should follow local recording-consent laws before recording phone calls.

Is TicNote worth it?

TicNote may be worth it for people who frequently record meetings, calls, lectures, or interviews and need transcripts, summaries, translations, and searchable notes.

Is TicNote private?

TicNote has a published privacy policy, but privacy depends on how recordings, transcripts, accounts, cloud processing, and user settings are handled.
